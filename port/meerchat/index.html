What does it do?

Meerchat is an app for UF students to connect!

  • Post a short video of yourself that only nearby peers can see
  • Propose an activity you want to meet up and do
  • Use a #hashtag so others can find you
  • When someone wants to join, they respond with a private video of their own
  • Choose who to respond to, opening up a private chat so you can plan your meet up
  • Public videos only last 24 hours
